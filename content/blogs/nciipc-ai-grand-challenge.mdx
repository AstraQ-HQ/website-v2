We are excited to announce that **AstraQ Cyber Defence Pvt Ltd** has been **selected among the top 6 teams** for the prestigious **NCIIPC Startup India AI Grand Challenge** under _PS-4: Retrieval Augmented Generation based Question and Answering System_. Out of **103 total submissions**, only **15 teams were invited to present at IIT Delhi**, and we are proud to be among the **final 6 innovators recognized for excellence and ingenuity**.

## About the Challenge

The **NCIIPC Startup India AI Grand Challenge** aims to promote cutting-edge solutions addressing critical national needs through deep tech innovation. The challenge invited submissions from startups, researchers, and tech pioneers to solve complex AI problem statements designed to strengthen capabilities in areas such as **cybersecurity, natural language understanding, and advanced information systems**.

Under **Problem Statement 4 (PS-4)** - _Retrieval Augmented Generation based Question and Answering System_ participants were tasked with building an **intelligent AI system** that combines high-quality information retrieval with generative reasoning to answer queries accurately over complex datasets. This challenge integrates the latest advancements in **semantic search, context-aware AI responses, and real-time knowledge synthesis**.

## Our Approach and Solution

Our team developed a **Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG)** based question-answering system tailored to deliver **contextual, precise, and explainable answers** from large and diverse document repositories. Key aspects of our solution include:

- **Semantic Document Retrieval**: Leveraging vector embeddings to identify relevant content with high contextual similarity.
- **Dynamic Knowledge Fusion**: Combining retrieved information with generative AI to produce accurate, source-grounded responses.
- **Scalable Architecture**: Designed for agility across varied data sources and formats, ensuring reliability in real-world deployments.

Through rigorous testing and iterative refinement, our system demonstrated **robust performance in delivering relevant answers** even in scenarios requiring deep contextual understanding and inference.
